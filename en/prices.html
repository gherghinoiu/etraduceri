Prices

The price for a translation takes into account the language pair, the size of the document, the field or difficulty of the text, the complexity of the format and the desired delivery date (e.g. urgent translation). Laseda Services' prices are among the most competitive on the market.

- Standard translation page = 2000 characters, including spaces.

- A minimum of one page is charged for documents under 2000 characters.

- for translations carried out on an urgent basis (less than 24 hours), or on weekends/public holidays, a charge of 50% of the price per page is charged.

- for proofreading/correcting a translation page, a charge of 50% of the price per page is payable.

- for each extra copy, we charge a fee of 3 lei / page.

- the cost for delivery of the translation by post / courier will be borne by the client.

How it works

1. Send us the document or text to be translated, the language(s) you want to translate into, the deadline and all order details.

2. We will provide you with a quotation and delivery date as soon as possible.

3. After your confirmation, the translation/proofreading/interpreting services will be carried out.

4. Delivery will be made as soon as possible, electronically by e-mail and/or physically by courier to the requested physical address.

 
 

Our services are aimed at both companies and individuals, our defining criteria being professionalism and flexibility. A satisfied customer is a clear proof of our success.
