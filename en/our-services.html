Authorized translations

Authorized translations are translations carried out by translators authorized by the Romanian Ministry of Justice to translate official documents and to work with all institutions within this ministry, or with other state institutions, such as the police, courts, law firms or notary offices.

 

Certified translations

The certified translation is, as in the case of the authorized translation, carried out by an authorized translator and then signed and stamped by a notary public, who certifies that the translator is indeed authorized and entitled to carry out the translation.

In Romania, the translation can be certified by a notary public, who certifies, in addition to the translator's signature and stamp, that the document that has been translated is an original document. The latter is not responsible for the accuracy of the translation.

 

Proofreading services
